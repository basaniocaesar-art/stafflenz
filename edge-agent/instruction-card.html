Quick Setup — 2 minutes

  1. Connect the power cable to the white port on the device.
  2. Plug the other end into a wall socket near your WiFi router.
    The device needs to be within range of your WiFi. Near the router is best.
  3. Wait 3–5 minutes. The device will automatically connect to your WiFi and start monitoring.
    You'll see a solid green light when it's connected.
  4. Log in to your dashboard at lenzai.org/login using the email and password we sent you.
Important: Do not unplug the device while it's running. It monitors your site 24/7 and needs constant power. If you lose power, the device will restart automatically when power returns.
